Tuna Pesto

Ingredients

  • Canned Tuna
  • Dry Penne Pasta
  • Fresh or Canned Pesto Sauce
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil
  • Water

Preparation

  1. Prepare a 1L-1.5L pot or sauce pan with a small amount of olive oil and 600mL of room temperature water.
  2. Place the pot or pan over high heat until it comes to a roaring boil.
  3. Add a pinch of salt and 250g of penne pasta or a pasta of your choice.
  4. Maintain high heat until pasta is tender.
  5. Remove and drain pasta, saving approximately 50ml of the water.
  6. Place the remaining water and canned tuna lightly drained in the pot or pan and heat at a medium low temperature for approximately one minute.
  7. Add pesto to the tuna in the pot or pan and heat until the oil in the pesto begins to bubble.
  8. Add the drained pasta to the pot or pan and mix it into the tuna pesto sauce until it is covered.
  9. Turn off the stove and let pesto and pasta mixture sit to absorb flavor for aproximately one to five minutes
  10. Remove from stove and plate while still hot. Add parmesan or a desired cheese.
